Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential projects are completed within 3 to 5 days depending on scope, weather, and roofing system.

How do I know if I need a new roof?

Common signs include active leaks, recurring repairs, curling shingles, moss growth, and visible wear around flashings, valleys, and vents.

What should I verify before hiring a roofer in BC?

Ask for WorkSafeBC coverage verification, proof of liability insurance, written scope and warranty terms, and clear permit responsibility for your municipality.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Stonnex is fully licensed and bonded, carries liability insurance, and works with WorkSafeBC-compliant safety standards.

Do you provide written warranties?

Yes. We provide written manufacturer and workmanship warranty documentation based on the selected roofing system and project scope.
